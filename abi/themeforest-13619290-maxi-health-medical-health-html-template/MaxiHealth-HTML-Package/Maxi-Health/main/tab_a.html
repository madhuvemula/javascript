Pre operative planning

  • On Admission Routine Blood Tests – Hb% , CBC, Blood Sugar, Blood Group Type, KFT, LFT, Serology, Urine Routine will be done.
  • Physician will examine the patient.
  • ECG and ECHO (if required) will be done and Cardiologist will check the patient for anesthesia fitness.
  • Gynecologist opinion will be obtained as and when required.
  • Anesthetist will do a complete pre -anesthetic evaluation of the patient.
  • Dental Examination will be obtained as and when required.
  • Blood and Blood Components are arranged depending upon the patient’s requirement.
  • Once the patient is fit for anesthesia and surgery implant / prosthesis options are given to the patient.
